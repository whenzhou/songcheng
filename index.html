Frequently Asked Questions

What is your MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity)?

Standard MOQ is 50 pcs per SKU. For trial orders, we accept as low as 1-2 pcs for sample evaluation. Bulk orders enjoy tiered pricing — contact us for a quote.

What is your lead time?

Standard production lead time is 30 days. Expedited 15-day service is available for urgent orders. Samples ship within 3 business days.

Do you charge for samples?

Yes — sample fee = product cost + shipping (typically $30-80 via DHL/FedEx depending on destination). 100% refundable against your first bulk order. Custom OEM samples may require a one-time setup fee. Samples ship within 3 business days.

What is the OEM/ODM mold development cycle?

New mold development typically takes 25-35 days from confirmed drawings to first samples. We provide 3D design review within 7 days. Existing molds can be adapted in 10-15 days.
